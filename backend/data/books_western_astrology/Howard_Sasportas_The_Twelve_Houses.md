# The Twelve Houses
## Author: Howard Sasportas

### Section: The First House
The 1st House is the lens through which we view the world and how the world sees us. Planets here strongly color the personality and physical body.
- **Sun in 1st house**: A strong need to be noticed; a radiant, vital presence. The self is the main project.
- **Moon in 1st house**: Emotional transparency. The person wears their heart on their sleeve and is highly reactive to the environment.

### Section: The Second House
The 2nd House deals with resources, self-worth, and material security.
- **Venus in 2nd house**: Attracts wealth easily, values beautiful possessions, ties self-worth to material stability.

### Section: The Tenth House
The 10th House relates to career, public standing, and the "father" archetype (authority).
- **Sun in 10th house**: Ambition is central to the identity. A natural authority figure who seeks a prominent place in society.
- **Saturn in 10th house**: Heavy sense of responsibility regarding career. Success comes late but is solidly built. Fear of failure drives ambition.
