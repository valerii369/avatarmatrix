# Saturn: A New Look at an Old Devil
## Author: Liz Greene

### Section: Introduction to Saturn
Saturn represents our deepest fears, our defense mechanisms, and the area of life where we feel inadequate. Yet, it is exactly through facing these fears that we build unshakeable inner strength.
- **Saturn in Aries**: Fear of asserting oneself. The task is to build courage step-by-step.
- **Saturn in Cancer**: Profound fear of emotional vulnerability and rejection. The task is to become one's own mother.
- **Saturn in Leo**: Fear of being ordinary or unrecognized. The task is to find inner validation rather than needing applause.

### Section: Saturn's Aspects
- **Saturn Square Sun**: A clash between the ego and necessity. The father figure may have been restrictive or absent. The person must learn self-reliance.
- **Saturn Trine Moon**: Emotional stability built over time. The person is a rock for others, having mastered their own emotional needs.
