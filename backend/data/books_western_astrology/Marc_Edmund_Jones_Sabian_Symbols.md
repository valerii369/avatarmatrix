# The Sabian Symbols in Astrology
## Author: Marc Edmund Jones

### Section: Symbols Overview
The Sabian Symbols provide a specific image for every degree of the zodiac, adding deep symbolic resonance to planet placements.
- **Aries 1**: "A woman just risen from the sea. A seal is embracing her." Meaning: Emergence of new life and the embrace of primal instinct.
- **Taurus 1**: "A clear mountain stream." Meaning: Pure, unpolluted resourcefulness and steadfast determination.
