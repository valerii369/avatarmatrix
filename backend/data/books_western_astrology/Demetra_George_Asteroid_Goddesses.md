# Asteroid Goddesses
## Author: Demetra George

### Section: Ceres, Pallas, Juno, Vesta and Lilith
- **Black Moon Lilith**: Represents the exiled feminine, primal rage, and unapologetic sexuality. Where Lilith sits is where we refuse to compromise, where we experience shaming, and where we must reclaim our wild power.
- **Juno**: The archetype of the committed partner. Indicates what we need in a long-term relationship, distinct from what we romantically desire (Venus).
