# The Nodes of the Moon
## Author: Bernadette Brady

### Section: Nodes in Houses
- **South Node in 1st, North Node in 7th**: The soul is accustomed to independence, selfishness, and solo battles. The evolutionary goal is to learn compromise, partnership, and seeing through another's eyes.
- **South Node in 10th, North Node in 4th**: The soul is accustomed to public duty and repressing emotions for the sake of career. The goal is to build a private life, emotional roots, and family.
