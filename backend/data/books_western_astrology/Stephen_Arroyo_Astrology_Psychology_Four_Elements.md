# Astrology, Psychology and the Four Elements
## Author: Stephen Arroyo

### Section: Element Balance
The elemental balance indicates the basic psychological temperament.
- **Fire Dominant**: High energy, visionary, spontaneous. Risk of burnout or lacking ground.
- **Earth Dominant**: Practical, grounded, materially focused. Risk of rigidity and lacking imagination.
- **Air Dominant**: Intellectual, communicative, objective. Risk of living entirely in the head and disconnecting from feelings.
- **Water Dominant**: Emotional, empathetic, intuitive. Risk of being overwhelmed by the environment and losing boundaries.
- **Missing Elements**: A missing element indicates a blind spot, a psychological "hunger". A lack of Earth means difficulty dealing with the material world; a lack of Water means difficulty processing grief and empathy.
