# Chiron and the Healing Journey
## Author: Melanie Reinhart

### Section: Chiron in the Signs and Houses
Chiron represents the wound that will not heal, the area where we feel chronically inadequate, but also where we have the greatest capacity to heal others.
- **Chiron in Aries**: The wound of existence and the right to be. Healing comes through fighting for the vulnerable.
- **Chiron in Virgo**: The wound of perfectionism and bodily control. Healing comes through accepting the imperfect mind/body connection.
- **Chiron in the 1st House**: The wound is highly visible. The person may feel like an alien. Healing comes through accepting one's unique, flawed identity.
