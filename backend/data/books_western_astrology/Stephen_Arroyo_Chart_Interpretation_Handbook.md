# Chart Interpretation Handbook
## Author: Stephen Arroyo

### Section: The Sun
The Sun represents the core identity, the conscious ego, and the vital life force.
- **Sun in Aries**: Driven by an urge to act and assert the self. Independent, pioneering, sometimes impulsive.
- **Sun in Taurus**: Driven by an urge for stability and sensual security. Steadfast, patient, resourceful.
- **Sun in Gemini**: Driven by intellectual curiosity and a need to communicate. Versatile, restless, observant.
- **Sun in Cancer**: Driven by emotional security and a need to nurture. Sensitive, protective, clinging to the past.
- **Sun in Leo**: Driven by a need for creative self-expression and recognition. Dramatic, loyal, proud.
- **Sun in Virgo**: Driven by a need to serve and improve. Analytical, precise, critical.
- **Sun in Libra**: Driven by a need for harmony and relationship. Diplomatic, aesthetic, indecisive.
- **Sun in Scorpio**: Driven by a need for deep emotional intensity and transformation. Penetrating, secretive, powerful.
- **Sun in Sagittarius**: Driven by a quest for truth and meaning. Philosophical, adventurous, blunt.
- **Sun in Capricorn**: Driven by a need for structure, achievement, and authority. Disciplined, ambitious, cautious.
- **Sun in Aquarius**: Driven by a need for innovation and social progress. Unconventional, detached, humanitarian.
- **Sun in Pisces**: Driven by a need to transcend boundaries through empathy. Compassionate, imaginative, escapist.

### Section: The Moon
The Moon represents emotional needs, instinctual reactions, and the unconscious.
- **Moon in Aries**: Needs quick emotional release; reacts with impatience but forgets anger quickly.
- **Moon in Taurus**: Needs physical comfort and predictability; reacts slowly but steadily.
- **Moon in Cancer**: Needs to feel emotionally safe and nurtured; highly attuned to moods and atmosphere.

### Section: Mercury
- **Retrograde Mercury meaning**: When Mercury is retrograde, the mental energy is directed inward. It indicates a mind that processes information in a highly subjective, contemplative way. Often there is a delay in expressing thoughts, but a profound capacity for re-evaluating and deep thinking.
